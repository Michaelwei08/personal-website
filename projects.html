What makes a project portfolio-ready?

  1. A real question and a reason the answer matters.
  2. An inspectable artifact, workflow, or analysis rather than a technology list.
  3. Evidence, including negative results and counterexamples.
  4. A clearly named next experiment instead of pretending the work is finished.
